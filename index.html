What I do help

01. User Research

user research experience involves using various methods to gather insights from users, observing them interact with the product, analysing the data, and making data-driven decisions to improve the overall user experience. The experience highlighted the importance of putting users at the centre of the design process and using their feedback to inform product development. The user research journey was a rewarding and inspiring experience that taught the importance of continuous learning and growth as a UX researcher.

02. Lo-fi and Hi-fi designs

have experience working with both lo-fi and hi-fi designs. Lo-fi designs are ideal for quick ideation and exploration, while hi-fi designs are useful for showcasing design details and communicating design intent to stakeholders and developers. I value both types of designs and uses them interchangeably based on the project's needs.

03. Mockup, testing and development

have experience creating UI/UX mockups and conducting testing to ensure user-friendliness. Mockups serve as a blueprint for the interface, and user testing helps to gather feedback for design iterations. It's a rewarding experience to see design improvements based on user feedback, and it has helped me to understand the importance of user-centred design. I also partake in development activities such as building websites with react.js, html and css
